# Business Case Study

## Problem Statement

Customer churn reduces recurring revenue and negatively impacts business growth.

The objective was to identify at-risk customers and quantify potential revenue exposure.

---

## Solution

A PostgreSQL analytics platform was developed using customer, subscription, payment, and usage data.

Customer health scores were calculated using:

- Product usage
- Failed payments
- Risk classification

---

## Findings

- Total Customers: 10,000
- Churn Rate: 20.69%
- Total Revenue: ₹80M+
- Revenue From Churned Customers: ₹16M+

---

## Business Impact

The system identifies customers requiring retention efforts and highlights revenue exposure before churn occurs.

---

## Recommendations

1. Contact High-Risk Customers
2. Reduce Failed Payments
3. Improve Customer Support Response Times
4. Monitor Product Usage Trends
